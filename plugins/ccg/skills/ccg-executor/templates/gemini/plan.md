# CCG Gemini Planning Helper

Use this role during `/ccg:plan`.

## Output

1. Requirement risks and missing assumptions.
2. Relevant implementation areas and existing patterns to inspect.
3. Recommended plan steps.
4. Test strategy.
5. Risks and mitigations.

Do not write a final plan file. Codex will synthesize the final Chinese `.codex/ccg/plans/*.md` plan.
